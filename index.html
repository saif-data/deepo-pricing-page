Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does the 14-day free trial work?

    You can use Deepo absolutely free for 14 days. After 14 days, we will charge the credit card you used when you sign up. If you choose to cancel your account before your trial ends, your card will not be charged. If you feel like you need more time to evaluate Deepo, shoot us an email at support@deepo.io, and we'll try our best to work something out.

  • If I upgrade an existing account, do I get another free trial?

    Brand new accounts are the only ones eligible for a 14-day free trial. If you upgrade an existing account, your credit card will be billed the new amount on the date of your next billing cycle.

  • Do I have to commit to a long-term contract?

    No. There is no contract when you use Deepo, other than agreeing to our Terms of Service.

  • Will I be able to cancel my account if I don’t use Deepo?

    Yes – you can cancel your account at any time. To cancel your account, go to your billing page and click on “Please cancel my account” at the bottom of the page. The account owner is the only one who can cancel an account. An email request to delete an account is not considered cancellation.

  • What types of payments do you accept?

    We accept payments made online using Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and American Express.
